Mission

Service to those who serve — and everyone they protect.

Our mission is to eliminate preventable deaths from trauma and hemorrhagic shock by advancing innovative research, developing field-ready treatments, and rapidly translating military medical breakthroughs into lifesaving solutions.

We focus on the full continuum of care: from point of injury, through evacuation and resuscitation, to definitive treatment.

Vision

A world where survivable shock is no longer fatal.

We envision a world where no soldier or civilian dies from survivable shock—where science, technology, and systems of care work together to deliver rapid, effective solutions.

The Problem

Survivable hemorrhagic shock still kills thousands every year.

Uncontrolled bleeding remains a leading cause of preventable death after trauma.

On the Battlefield

Medics make life-and-death decisions with limited diagnostics and minimal resources.

In Civilian Emergencies

EMS and emergency departments face unpredictable scenes and variable access to care.

In Research & Development

Traditional structures excel at discovery but struggle with sustained translation.
